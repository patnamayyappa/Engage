About Student Groups

Student group records enable you to perform an action or a process with students who belong to a group. Student Groups are a special type of Marketing List, but a Marketing List need not be a Student Group.

With the help of Azure functions, a supervisor can perform any of the following tasks with a student group:

  • Assign a success plan record
  • Associate a success network record
  • Manage student retention

Student groups can be:

  • Static: Students are manually added and removed from the group by user intervention
  • Dynamic: students are added to the group dynamically based on conditions in a filter

Student group records can be created to address scenarios as diverse as assigning representative duties, scheduling repeat lectures or managing schedules for basketball trails.

By default, the status of a student group is Active, i.e., it is available to perform an activity with a group of students.
