About Success Network Assignments

A success network assignment record is created with the objective of assigning a staff role (faculty member, coach, advisor, etc.) to a user. The record is also associated with a student group. When background logic then executes, the user is associated with each student in the group in the capacity of the role that is assigned.

Example

The Asian Languages Department at Seagull University creates the Upgrade Now student group which is associated with a success network assignment record in which you are assigned the staff role of Coach. Students in the group are enrolled into the Asian Languages program.

As coach, its your objective to assist native English speaking students to understand and then grow their knowledge of the history of East Asian languages. As the program requires students to begin reading and writing elementary Japanese by the second semester, apart from helping students upgrade their skills, it also equips them for reading and writing assignments that will begin in the second semester.
