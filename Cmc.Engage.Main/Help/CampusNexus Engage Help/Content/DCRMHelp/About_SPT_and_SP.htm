About Success Planning

Success planning is a framework in which members of faculty and students collaborate to formulate student plans and activities. Success planning can happen based on student grades, academic goals, student self-skilling targets, completing specific tasks, and so on. Examples when success plans can be formulated are:

  • When student grades are below a threshold score, faculty can assign success plans
  • When students are keen to gain additional skills in a specific area
  • When students are keen to learn new skills that are not included in their curriculum
About Success Plan Templates and Success Plans

Faculty or student advisors can create success plan template or success plan records. A success plan is a group of activities assigned to a specific student and the advisor. Success plans can be created with or without a success plan template.

A success plan template is a reusable group of activities that can be assigned to multiple students. When it's assigned to a student, a new success plan record is created for the student with the latterâ€™s name appended to the success plan template name. After assignment, both the success plan template and success plan can be modified without affecting each other.

About Success Plan To Do Templates and To Dos

A success plan to do template is a template of an activity to be performed. The To Do is the actual activity which will be performed.

When the success plan template (including its success plan to do template) is assigned to a student, a copy of the success plan to do template is automatically created, which is a To Do record.

To Do records can also be created in scenarios outside of success plan records when a task needs to be assigned to a student and it's not required to create a success plan as an associated record.

Success plan to do templates are reusable activities that can be assigned to a student. A To Do is a specific activity that a student or advisor will complete. After assignment, unique changes can be made to either record without affecting the related record.

Exception Scenarios

Success plans can be created independently (outside of success plan templates) in scenarios where there is a specific need, i.e., the scenario being addressed by the student advisor and / or the student is unique. Similarly, To Do records can be created outside of success plan records when a specific task needs to be assigned to a student and it's not required to create a success plan as an associated record. To Dos can be added to an existing success plan even if the success plans are created from a success plan template.
