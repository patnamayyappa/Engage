About Travel Management

The travel management module is used by recruiters (users in the system) who are assigned the task of enrolling students at a university. In this process, recruiters are assisted by volunteers (typically existing students) and other staff members. The process of student recruitment happens when recruiters and their teams of staff and volunteers organize seminars and / or attend events to promote their institution.

In a typical travel management implementation, a recruiter creates a trip record in which details of staff and volunteers are added. To enable owners to carry out their tasks, a trip is segmented into trip activities that can include travel and non-travel tasks. For example, promotional events that owners must conduct or subsidiary trips that recruiters need to make.

After creating a trip record, a recruiter needs to seek approval from an approval authority.

Travel management thus helps an institution to plan their trips to meet and enroll prospective students. Additionally, over multiple admission cycles, recruitment teams evolve their strategies by refining their travel management plans to achieve targeted admission numbers, and simultaneously manage their expenses optimally.

Travel management involves working with records of the following entities:

  • Trips
  • Trip Activities
