Assigning a Success Plan

When success plans are assigned to students, it enables them to complete tasks described in associated To Do records. Success plans can be assigned:

  • To multiple students
  • Individually to a student
  • To a group of students
To Assign a Success Plan to Multiple Students
  1. Select the check box next to student records to whom you want to assign a success plan.
  2. Click Assign Success Plan.
  3. In the Assign Success Plan dialog, type the name of the success plan record. The name of the record is resolved and displayed if it's available in the database.
  4. Click Assign.
To Assign a Success Plan to an Individual Student
  1. Select the check box next to the individual student's record.
    OR
    Open the student record.
  2. Click Assign Success Plan.
  3. In the Assign Success Plan dialog, type the name of the success plan record. The name of the record is resolved and displayed if it's available in the database.
  4. Click Assign.
To Assign a Success Plan to a Student Group
  1. In the form to create a success plan assignment, type valid values in mandatory and other fields and then save the completed record.
  2. In the Student Groups area, search for and select the student group to which the success plan needs to be assigned.
    The success plan is assigned to all students in the group when logic that executes at periodic intervals verifies that assignment criteria are fulfilled. For example, the assignment criteria can be that students must be:
    • In the South campus
    • In their sophomore year
    • Studying Advanced Mathematics as one of their major subjects

    Success plans and their associated To Dos are assigned to students who fulfill assignment criteria.

On assignment of a success plan, the following changes occur:

  • The success plan and To Do records are displayed in their respective grids and are accessible from the site map.
  • As the success plan and To Do records are assigned to a student, the records are displayed in the Student Success Profile area of the student's record.
