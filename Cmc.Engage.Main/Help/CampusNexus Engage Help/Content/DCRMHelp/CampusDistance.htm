About Miles to Campus

The Miles to Campus field in a Contact displays the distance of the selected University from the primary address of a Contact. The primary address of the Contact and the primary address of the University is used to calculate and automatically update the value of the Miles to Campus field. The Miles to Campus is recalculated if the primary address of the Contact or the University is updated.
