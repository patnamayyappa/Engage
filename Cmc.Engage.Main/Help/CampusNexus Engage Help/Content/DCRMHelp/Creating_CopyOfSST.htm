Creating a Copy of a Staff Survey Template

You can create a copy of a Staff Survey Template to reduce the effort of creating a Staff Survey Template afresh.

Steps to Create a Copy of a Staff Survey Template

  1. Go to Staff Survey Template.

  2. Select a Staff Survey Template from the list view.

  3. Select the Staff Survey Template menu option as shown below.

  4. Click .

  5. Click OK in the message that is displayed.

    A copy of the selected Staff Survey Template is created.
