Creating Success Plan Templates and Success Plan To Do Templates

As a member of faculty or as a student advisor, you can create success plan templates. These are created to function as containers or shells. They are referred to as success plan templates as they are assigned to a group of students in scenarios where student tasks are repetitive or if they occur to a large section of the student community at the same time. Typically, success plan templates are just that, templates that contain time-bound tasks (also known as success plan to do templates) to address student scenarios. Based on repetitive scenarios that occurred previously, student advisors can create success plan template records and their associated success plan to do templates. As the records are already created, advisors simply need to assign them to students to direct them to complete the required tasks.

Note

In the success plan to do template, perform the following steps to set a value in the Due Date Calculation Type field:

  • If you select Static:
    • Select a valid date in the Due Date-Static field.
  • If you select Calculated:
    1. Type a count of days in the Due Date Number of Days field.
    2. In the Due Date Days Direction field, select Before or After.
    3. In the Due Date Calculation field, select one of the following values:
      • Assignment Date
      • End of Academic Period
      • Start of Academic Period

      Example

      If you type 10 in the Due Date Number of Days field, select Before in the Due Date Days Direction field, and then select End of Academic Period, the due date will be 10 days before the end of the academic period.
