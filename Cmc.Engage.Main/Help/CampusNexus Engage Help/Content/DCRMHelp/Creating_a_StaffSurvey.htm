Creating a Staff Survey

Precondition: The Staff Survey can be created and assigned by any user, However, the sender name in the email is always the owner of the Staff Survey - Send Email process, who should have access to send email on behalf of a user who assigns a staff survey.

A Staff Survey can be created using one of the following methods:

  • Assign a Staff Survey Template to a Staff Course

  • Create a Staff Survey

Assign a Staff Survey Template to a Staff Course

A Staff Course record provides information about a course and the instructor associated to the course for an academic period in a school. When a Staff Survey Template is assigned to a Staff Course, a Staff Survey record is automatically created for the instructor of the Staff Course.

Use this method to assign a Staff Survey Template to multiple Staff Courses using a single step.

Steps to Assign a Staff Survey Template to a Staff Course

  1. Go to Staff Courses.

  2. Select Staff Course(s) and click Assign Staff Survey Template.

  3. In the dialog box, type the required Staff Survey Template name, and click Assign.

    Note: A Staff Survey Template can be assigned to a Staff Course only when the following conditions are met:

    • A Staff Survey with the same start and end date is not already assigned to the same instructor of the same staff course.

    • The start date of the Staff Survey is not prior to the current date.

    • The start date or due date should not fall between existing survey assigned to the instructor of the staff course.

Create a Staff Survey

Use this method to manually create a Staff Survey and assign a Staff Survey Template to a Staff Course.

Steps to create a Staff Survey

  1. Go to Staff Survey.

  2. Click New.

  3. Specify values in the following fields:

    • Staff Survey Name
    • Staff Course
    • Staff Survey Template

  4. Click Save.

Once a Staff Survey is created for an instructor, the instructor receives an email notification on the Start Date of the Staff Survey. The Start Date of the Staff Survey is obtained from the Start Date configured in the Staff Survey Template.

Further, if the instructor has not completed the staff survey, reminder emails are sent to the instructor before a specific number of days before due date. The number of days before the due date when the reminder email must be sent to an instructor can be configured in the StaffSurveySendREminderEmailNumberOfDays configuration (under Settings > Configurations).

Note: The status of a Staff Survey can be determined using the Status Reason property of the Staff Survey. The Status Reason of a staff survey becomes Overdue if the Staff Survey is not completed on or after the due date in the system.
