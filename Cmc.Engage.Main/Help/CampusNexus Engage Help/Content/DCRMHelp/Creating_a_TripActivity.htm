Creating a Trip Activity

Trip activities are tasks performed by a recruiter, other staff or volunteers. Multiple trip activities contribute to the overall completion of the parent trip.

As a recruiter, you need to create trip activity records to enable execution of the different subsidiary trips in a parent trip. The parent trip record is essentially a container that comprises these subsidiary trips. These are referred to as trip activity records and contain details of individual trips that contribute to the completion of the parent trip. Trip activity records include the following information:

  • Start and end date details
  • Location information
  • Appointment details
  • Travel and expense details
  • Details of staff members and volunteers who will execute or help in the execution of the trip activity
