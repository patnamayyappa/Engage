Associating an Image URL With a Staff Survey Template Record

  1. Go to Settings > Customizations > Customize the System.

  2. In the left pane, click Web Resources and click New.

  3. In the Name field, type /Images/ and then suffix the name of the image.

    The typed text will be included in the URL when you save the record.

  4. Type appropriate values in other fields.

  5. Click Choose File to select an image to upload.

  6. Click Save.

    The URL of the selected image is displayed in the URL field.

  7. Click Publish.

  8. Copy the URL.

Inserting the URL in a Staff Survey Template Record

  1. Open a new Staff Survey Template record.

  2. Specify appropriate values in the fields.

  3. In the Description area, click .

  4. In the Web address field, copy the saved URL and then click Insert.

  5. Click Save.
    The saved image will appear in the header area of the Staff Survey Template record in the preview section in the Staff Survey Template designer form and in the feedback form.
