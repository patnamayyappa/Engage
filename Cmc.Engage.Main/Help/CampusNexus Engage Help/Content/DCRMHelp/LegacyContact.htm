Legacy Student

The Legacy field in a Contact indicates that the student is a legacy student meaning they have had a family member in the past who attended the same university that they are or will be attending.

The Legacy field is automatically selected when the following conditions are met and the StopLegacyCheck configuration record is on:

  • A family connection exists between the legacy contact and the family member contact.

  • Family member contact has an alumni connection to an account with an account type of campus.

  • The alumni connection and family connection are active.

Note: The StopLegacyCheck configuration record can be turned off to prevent automatic selection of the Legacy field when the above conditions are met.
