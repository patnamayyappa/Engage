Predicting Student Retention

This feature uses Machine Learning and helps an institution predict a student's retention percentage based on factors such as the student's age, gender, location (whether the student stays off or on campus), GPA, and so on. The approximate retention percentage helps administrators plan events in student retention activities. In institutions with large retention databases, retention percentages across a large section of students can serve as a mechanism to predict if future investments in academic programs or infrastructure are required.

To Predict Student Retention
  1. In a student record, click On Demand Prediction.
    The following message will be displayed: Retention Probability was successfully updated.
  2. Navigate to the Student Success Profile area. The retention percentage will be displayed in the Cognitive Model Based Retention Probability field.
