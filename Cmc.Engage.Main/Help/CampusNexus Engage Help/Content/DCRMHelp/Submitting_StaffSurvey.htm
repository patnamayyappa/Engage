Viewing and Submitting a Staff Survey

When a Staff Survey is created and assigned to an instructor, an email notification is sent to the instructor on the start date of the Staff Survey. The instructor can view the Staff Survey by clicking the link in the email notification or from the My Survey option in the Faculty application.

Steps to view and submit a Staff Survey

  1. Open the Faculty application.

  2. Navigate to My Surveys.

  3. Click a Staff Survey from the list view.

  4. Answer the survey questions for each student and click Save or Submit.

    Clicking the Save button allows the instructor to save a draft of the staff survey and update it later and submit it. However, clicking the Submit button will complete the survey and the form cannot be edited. The instructor can only view the submitted staff surveys.
