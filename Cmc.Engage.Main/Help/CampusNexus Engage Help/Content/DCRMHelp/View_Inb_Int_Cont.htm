Viewing Inbound Interest Details in a Contact

Inbound interest records are displayed in different areas of a contact record:

In the Inbound Interests area of a contact record:

By default, inbound interest records associated with the contact are displayed in this grid.

In the Initial Source area

This information is read-only and displays details of the inbound interest record in which the Primary option is selected. Staff can collate this information from inbound interest records created in a time frame and then analyze information such as:

  • The count of students who changed their initial program
  • The course in which students are currently enrolled as compared to the initial choice that students made.
  • The duration after which students changed their course.

This analysis helps admission coordinators modify or realign their admission processes.

In the Marketing area:

Refresh the field to view the date and time of the most recently created inbound interest record.

Analysis of this field across a student segment helps staff to gauge the count of records in a specific date range. They can then determine if lifecycle records need to be created for specific inbound interests.
